About Rialo

Rialo, developed by Subzero Labs, is designed to seamlessly connect Web2 and Web3. With event-driven architecture, Rialo enables smart contracts to interact with real-world data securely and efficiently.

Key Features

  • Native Web2 Integration with APIs
  • Social & Email Login without traditional wallets
  • High Speed & Low Latency transactions
  • VM Compatibility: Solana VM & RISC-V
